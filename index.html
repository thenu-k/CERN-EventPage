COMPELLING SCIENCE COMMUNICATION is an essential skill to share knowledge and inspire change. With MSc25, we provide you the opportunity to practice what it means to be a great physicist.

We invite participants from all over the world to produce and upload short videos that break down a physics concepts of your choosing to a general audience. This is an exercise that will develop both your understaning of the subject and your communication skills. The competition is intended for you to learn new physics and share your findings. We do not favour complexity or production value over effective explanations.

PRIZES

Participants wil be randomly chosen to explore CERN in an exclusive virutal tour.

Participants who produce exceptional videos will have their videos promoted on our platform. They will also have the exciting opportunity to meet a scientist at CERN.

RULES

Submissions will open on the 20th of June 0000h CEST and end on the 4th of July 0000h CEST (Higgs Boson day). To get a better idea of what is expected, we have produced example submissions below.

All participants must adhere to the following rules to be considered for a prize.

  1. The content must be physics related and must explain a concept to a general audience.
  2. The video can be 30 seconds to 5 minutes long. Concise explanations are preferred.
  3. The video will be publicly uploaded to Instagram (as a post or reel), TikTok, or YouTube (as a short or video).
  4. Graphics, and props are welcome but not necessary.
  5. You can submit a video on a topic that has been covered by another submission. This will not count as a penalization. Your submission will be judged independently.
  6. The participant must complete this short form, and attach a link to their video.
