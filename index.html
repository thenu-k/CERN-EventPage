Midsummer Science Competition

MSc25

COMPELLING SCIENCE COMMUNICATION is an essential tool to share your passion with the world, and a powerful exercise to develop your own understanding of the subject. MSc25 is a competition inspired by this need for communication.

We invite participants from all over the world to produce and upload short videos that breaks down physics concepts of your choosing to a general audience. The competition is intended for you to learn new physics and share your findings. We do not favour complexity or production value over effective explanations.

RULES

Submissions will open on the 20th of June and end on the 4th of July (Higgs Boson day). The range of topics is flexible but we expect the video to be related to physics. To get a better idea of what is expected, please see this video.

Do not be discouraged by concepts that you might find intimidating. Learn about the topic that you are passionate about and share what you understand about it. What made it click? The true value of shared knowledge lies in this diversity. And most importantly, have fun.

  1. The video will be publicly uploaded to Instagram (as a post or reel), TikTok, or YouTube (as a short or video).
  2. The participant must complete this short form, and attach a link to their video.
  3. The content must be physics related or adjacent, and must explain a concept to a general audience.
  4. Graphics, and props are welcome but not necessary.
  5. You can submit a video on a topic that has been covered by another submission. This will not count as a penalization. Your submission will be judged independently.
